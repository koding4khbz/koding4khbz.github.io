Privacy Policy for JPG Converter

Introduction

Welcome to JPG Converter. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your data.

Information We Collect

Images and Files

  • Local Processing Only: All images you select for conversion are processed entirely on your device.
  • No Upload: We do NOT upload, store, or transmit your images to any external servers.
  • Temporary Storage: Images are temporarily stored in your device's cache during the conversion process and are automatically deleted afterward.

App Preferences

  • Language Settings: Your selected language preference is stored locally on your device.
  • App Settings: Any customization settings are saved locally on your device only.

Tips and Support (Buy Me a Coffee)

  • Third-Party Processing: All tip processing is handled by "Buy Me a Coffee" (buymeacoffee.com).
  • No Direct Collection: We do NOT directly collect or store your payment card information.
  • Transaction Data: Buy Me a Coffee may share limited transaction information (such as your name/email if you choose to provide it) for acknowledgment purposes only.
  • Optional: Leaving a tip is entirely voluntary and not required for any app functionality.
  • One-Time Only: Tips are one-time payments, not recurring subscriptions.

No Other Personal Information

We do NOT collect, store, or process:

  • Names (except if voluntarily provided via Buy Me a Coffee)
  • Email addresses (except if voluntarily provided via Buy Me a Coffee)
  • Phone numbers
  • Precise location data
  • Browsing history
  • Payment card details

How We Use Your Information

Image Processing

  • Images are processed locally on your device
  • Converted files are stored temporarily in your device's storage
  • You have full control over sharing or deleting converted files

App Functionality

  • Language preferences are used to display the app in your chosen language
  • Settings are used to customize your app experience

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All images and conversion data are stored exclusively on your device
  • No cloud storage or external servers are used for image processing
  • You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app

Security Measures

  • All image processing happens locally on your device
  • Server communications use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Your images never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share them
  • Regular security audits and updates

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

  • The app can be safely used by users of all ages for image conversion
  • Donations require adult supervision and payment methods
  • If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately

Permissions

Required Permissions

Storage/Photos (Required)

  • Purpose: Access images from your gallery and save converted files
  • Usage: Only when you select images or save converted files
  • Control: You choose which images to access

Optional Permissions

Camera (Optional)

  • Purpose: Take photos for conversion
  • Usage: Only when you explicitly select "Camera" option
  • Not required: You can use the app without granting camera access

Data Retention

Local Data

  • Images are processed in real-time and not retained after conversion
  • Temporary files are automatically deleted after conversion
  • App settings remain until you uninstall the app

Donation Data

  • Managed by Buy Me a Coffee per their retention policies
  • We do not control or have access to this data
  • Contact Buy Me a Coffee for data deletion requests

International Users

Data Processing

  • Image processing occurs locally on your device (no international transfer)
  • Buy Me a Coffee processes payments according to their policies
